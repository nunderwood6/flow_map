Design Thinking

Overall: Humor is an underutilized design technique, and holds tremendous potential to help humanize branding.
Flow map: Emphasizes diversity and global influence of brand.
Colors: All colors are neutral or brand colors.
Sizing and Timing: Frame is sized to standard digital billboard aspect ratio, resizes responsively based on browser width. Sequence transitions are smooth and evenly timed.
Personal motivation: Do the folks I'll be working with have a sense of humor, and a critical self-lens?
