Design Thinking

Overall: Humor is an underutilized design technique, and holds tremendous potential to humanize brand.
Flow map: Emphasizes diversity and global influence of brand. Colors: All colors are neutral or brand colors. Sizing and Timing: Frame is sized to standard digital billboard size, resizes responsively when browser width changes. Sequence transitions smooth and evenly timed. Personal motivation: I don't want to work with people who don't have a sense of humor.
